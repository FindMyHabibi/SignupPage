A privacy focused matchmaking service for practicing Muslim Families and Individuals in Australia.

How much will it cost?

It's free for you, InshaAllah.

How does it work?

This service is run by sisters from Melbourne. We try to run it according to the Quran & Sunnah. We don't allow private chats or swiping on profiles, and we try to get the families involved from the beginning.

To get started, create a profile for yourself or for your family member. One month after you join us, we will give you access to anonymous profiles of all our active members. The profiles will not display names, pictures or contact details, but these can be requested at a later stage.

During the first month, we will try to compare your preferences with other members, this is before we give you access to all the profiles. If we find any potential matches, we will schedule a Zoom meeting with you to share more details about them. If you and your potential match want to proceed, then we will introduce you to each other over a Zoom call which will be supervised by our team, InshaAllah. During the initial meetings, we will try to protect your privacy by not sharing your contact details with your match.
